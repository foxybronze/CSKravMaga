Introduction for RAF Personnel

Pete gave an introduction to Krav Maga to an audience of RAF Personnel on Mon 07 Sep 2015. He delivered an interesting and physically relevant session adapting his coaching style to the abilities and strengths of the participants. His natural flair as an instructor allowed the session to be demanding and fun giving those involved an opportunity for some one on one coaching as well as group participation keeping the session varied and applicable. I would recommend his classes in Aylesbury to anyone wishing to develop an understanding of the principles and skills related to Krav Maga. Thank you Pete for your hard work and reminding us that if someone isn’t getting kicked in the A frame – it isn’t Krav Maga!

Sgt M Rodda, RAF Halton.

A Life Enhancing Experience!

The sessions at C S Krav Maga push you physically and mentally, but in a safe, friendly and supportive environment. Although the classes are mixed ability, all the members are there to learn, and help each other either with technique tips, or by being a good 'attacker'! No big egos here! I always leave feeling I have learned something new, stretched a few muscles, achieved things I didn't think I could, and had a great time doing it. I feel much more confident and aware in general, and look forward to my weekly sessions in the Hemel Hempstead location. Wouldn't be without it!

I have loved the transition from turning moves and mental approaches that were totally OUT of my comfort zone in to a state of mind and physical response that is now IN my comfort zone! It's been an amazing and life enhancing experience so far and I don't see myself stopping anytime soon. I am really happy I bit the bullet and gave it a go.

Heidi - Hemel Hempstead
